Finding Vets
To ensure that your pets stay healthy and happy, it's essential to take them to a veterinarian for regular check-ups.

We have embedded a link to a map with all the veterinary clinics in UAE. It will provide you with a list of many vets. This map shows you the vets that are nearest to you. If you are in Sharjah, it shows you the vets in Sharjah, but if you are in Amsterdam, it shows you the vets in Amsterdam.
How Often Should Your Pet See a Vet?
Your pet should be taken to the vet regularly, but how many times a year?

The answer depends on your pet's life stage.

Kitten or Puppy: Birth to 1 Year
You'll need to bring your little one in for vaccines every 3 to 4 weeks until they are 16 weeks old. They'll get vaccinations for different diseases and maybe medication.

Adult: 1 to 7-10 Years (depends on the breed)
In this gap, the doctors recommend yearly checkups for your pet, where they will check for diseases

Senior: 7 to 10 Years and Older
For pets of this age, vets suggest twice-a-year checkups. Your cat or dog will get vaccinations when needed and will get a thorough physical exam, along with tests to follow up on any problems.
